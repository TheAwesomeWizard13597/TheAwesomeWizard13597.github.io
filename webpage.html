Sensing

My robot would use the two ultrasonic sensors at the front of the dozer to sense objects in front of it.

Planning

After sensing the objects, the robot would plan based on whether or not there was an object there (for example, if there was an object there, turn, otherwise, keep going)

Acting

The robot would act using its six wheel tank tread driving forward. The plow attachment is completely passive and would stay stationary relative to the robot, and would be used to remove obstacles.
